Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Powel Mwenesi, Akubrecah Entertainment was established to meet the growing digital needs of Kapenguria and West Pokot County. We recognized the need for professional digital services in the region and committed to providing high-quality solutions.

Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality digital and printing services that empower businesses, students, and individuals in West Pokot County to achieve their goals.

Vision

To become the leading cybercafe and digital solutions provider in Western Kenya, known for reliability, quality, and excellent customer service.
